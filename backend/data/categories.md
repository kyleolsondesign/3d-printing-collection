# Category Definitions

This file defines what each category contains. It is used by the AI-powered ingestion tool to suggest categories for new models. Edit this file to improve categorization accuracy.

## 3D Printer Accessories

Parts, upgrades, and accessories for 3D printers themselves. Spool holders, filament guides, tool holders, bed clips, nozzle organizers, camera mounts for print monitoring.

## A1 Mini

Accessories and modifications specifically for the Bambu Lab A1 Mini printer.

## Apple Product Accessories

Cases, stands, mounts, docks, and accessories for Apple devices (iPhone, iPad, MacBook, Apple Watch, AirPods, etc.).

## Art, Architecture & Sculptures

Decorative art pieces, busts, sculptures, architectural models, wall art, relief prints, and display pieces.

## Bird Houses

Bird houses, birdhouse, bird feeders, and outdoor wildlife accessories.

## Cars

Model cars, vehicle accessories, car-related organizers, and automotive tools.

## Core One

Accessories and modifications specifically for the Prusa Core One 3D printer. "One Piece" has nothing to do with the Core One 3D printer.

## CyberBrick

CyberBrick building system pieces and accessories.

## Dragons

Dragon figurines, articulated dragons, dragon sculptures, and dragon-themed items.

## Drybox

Filament dry box designs, desiccant holders, and filament storage solutions for keeping filament dry.

## Electronics

Cases, mounts, and accessories for electronics: Raspberry Pi cases, cable management, USB holders, phone/tablet stands, speaker enclosures.

## Fidgets & Clickers

Fidget toys, clickers, spinners, stress relief toys, and tactile desk toys.

## Figurines

Character figurines, miniatures, statues, and collectible display models (non-dragon). Figurines are always characters, not objects.

## Game Night & Marble Runs

Board game accessories, card holders, dice towers, marble runs, game piece organizers, and tabletop gaming accessories.

## Gridfinity

Gridfinity modular storage system: bins, baseplates, holders, and custom inserts for the Gridfinity standard.

## HSW

HSW (Honeycomb Storage Wall) system pieces, mounts, and accessories.

## Kitchen & Cooking

Kitchen tools, utensils, organizers, spice racks, cup holders, fridge accessories, and cooking-related items. Includes coffee and tea items.

## Lamps & Lights

Lamp shades, light covers, LED holders, lithophanes, and lighting accessories.

## Nespresso

Nespresso coffee capsule holders, dispensers, and coffee machine accessories.

## Original Creations

My own original designs and creations. Category only assigned manually.

## PS5, Switch, Video Games

Gaming accessories: console stands, controller holders, game cartridge cases, headset mounts, and video game themed items.

## Paid

Models that were purchased or came from paid sources. Subcategorized by designer name. Only assigned manually.

## Patterns and Shapes

Geometric patterns, tessellations, mathematical shapes, and abstract geometric designs.

## Plants, Planters & Vases

Plant pots, planters, vases, self-watering systems, hydroponics, test tube planters, plant labels, and garden accessories.

## Skadis

IKEA Skadis pegboard accessories: hooks, holders, bins, and custom mounts for the Skadis system.

## Storage & Organization

General storage solutions, bowls, containers, bins, desk organizers, cable organizers, drawer dividers, wall mounts, hooks, and organizational accessories (not Gridfinity, HSW, or Skadis specific).

## Tea Lights & Candles

Tea light holders, candle holders, luminary designs, and decorative candle accessories.

## Tests

Test prints, calibration models, benchmarks (Benchy, temperature towers, etc.).

## Tools & Functional

Practical tools, wrenches, jigs, measuring tools, clamps, and other functional utility prints.

## Toys

Toys for children, playsets, action figures, building blocks, toy vehicles, and play accessories. Flexis and articulated models are most often toys.

## Wine & Coasters

Wine bottle holders, wine glass markers, coasters, drink accessories, and bar-related items.

## XMas

Christmas decorations, ornaments, tree toppers, advent calendars, and holiday-themed prints.
